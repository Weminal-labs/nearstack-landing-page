# The vision of Wemical

Wemical is a low-code DeFi platform providing multiple protocols and features powered by AI. With a diversified ecosystem of different products, we aim at 2 ultimate goals:

- Making investing easier and smarter: We use simple tools and AI to help you manage your investments better
- Improving how money moves: We offer two main services - Liquid Staking and Money Market - to help your money work harder for you

we are bringing more low-code DeFi products assisted by AI to users.
