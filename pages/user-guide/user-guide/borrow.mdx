# Participate In Liquidations
## Bid

1. Select sAssets to bid.

![Example Image](/assets/docs/bid1.png)

2. Choose the desired Premium to bid.
![Example Image](/assets/docs/bid2.png)


3. Input the amount of USDT to bid.
![Example Image](/assets/docs/bid3.png)


4. Click on [Place My Bid].
![Example Image](/assets/docs/bid1.png)


5. Bid complete.
![Example Image](/assets/docs/bid1.png)


6. Your bid will be shown at My Bids.
![Example Image](/assets/docs/bid1.png)
