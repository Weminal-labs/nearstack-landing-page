## Staked Token
sAssets are liquid, tokenized representations of staked (bonded) assets PoS blockchain. They provide stakeholders with liquidity over their staked assets, allowing the locked value of staked assets to be used in financial applications.

In Wemical Liquid Staking, there are a types of sAssets: 

- LiquidLP[TokenA-TokenB] (Staked Token)


With LiquidLP[TokenA-TokenB], holders can earn block rewards while maintaining liquidity and fungibility.

The difference between the two tokens is how they manage the staking rewards:


- LiquidLP[TokenA-TokenB] rewards are acquired rewards are re-staked, increasing the underlying amount of staked Token.
