# LiquidLP
## Staking
1. Input the desired amount of ORAI to be staked.

![Example Image](/assets/docs/staking1.png)
2. Click on [Stake].

![Example Image](/assets/docs/staking2.png)
3. Stake complete.

![Example Image](/assets/docs/staking3.png)
## Unstaking

1. Input the desired amount of scORAI to be unstake.

![Example Image](/assets/docs/staking4.png)
2. Click on ***[Unstake]***.

![Example Image](/assets/docs/staking5.png)
3. Request unstake complete.

![Example Image](/assets/docs/staking6.png)
4. Your request will be shown in Unstake requests.

![Example Image](/assets/docs/staking7.png)
5. ORAI will be claimable after 14-16 days and will be shown in Withdrawable. Click on [Withdraw] to receive ORAI.

![Example Image](/assets/docs/staking8.png)
